About Us CSC

CSC, or Common Service Center, is a transformative initiative aimed at bringing digital access and essential services to every corner of the nation. Established under the National e-Governance Plan, CSCs serve as community-driven service delivery points, offering a wide array of government and private services. From digital literacy to banking, healthcare, and more, CSCs act as a bridge between citizens and the digital world, fostering inclusivity, empowerment, and progress. Our commitment lies in making technology accessible and beneficial for all, contributing to the socio-economic development of our communities.

Internet Roshni

About Us Internet Roshni

Internet Roshni Digital Center is a program of the Digital Empowerment Foundation and Internet Society Foundation. This program focuses on access and inclusion of the Tea Tribe and Adivasi Community in Assam. The Adivasi Community in Assam has one of the lowest social and economic indicators. The Covid-19 pandemic afected the community as the production and demand decreased so the wages became irregular.With no steady, sustainable, affordable access to the internet, digital resources, opportunities, and welfare announcements, the community is further excluded and now faces challenges for response, recovery, and resilience
Centre location is: Serispore Tea,Near Kalimandir

Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at providing social security to workers in the unorganized sector. Under this scheme, eligible beneficiaries receive a monthly pension after attaining the age of 60, ensuring financial stability during their old age.Celebrate financial security with the Mandhan Yojana!
